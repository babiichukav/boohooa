Amazon Savings Hacks

  • Consistently save money on Amazon by signing up for Amazon Prime.
  • You can use any valid discount codes on any orders with Amazon, but most are product, category or brand specific.
  • Amazon's coupon section is a treasure trove of clippable coupons on dozens of categories and thousands of products.  Save up to 50% just by clicking Clip Coupon for any product of interest.
  • On some items, Amazon will reward you for not selecting expedited shipping options; a special discount might be applied to your order just for choosing the no-rush shipping option.
  • Students can get a 6-month free trial of Prime with the Amazon student discount program.
  • Amazon regularly offers digital coupons. Simply select the Clip Coupon option below the items you want, and when you select those items to add to your cart, your savings are automatically applied for you.
  • Using Amazons Save for Later option is a great way to save if you dont have to have the item immediately. Putting an item in your cart and then clicking save for later will prompt Amazon to send you alerts when prices change.
  • Amazon offers a Subscribe and Save program, which is an excellent way to save on household staples. Putting an item on your Subscribe and Save plan lets you decide how many you want of an item and how often you want it delivered, and you then automatically get 15% off that item.
  • Near the top of the screen, there is a tab marked Daily Deals where you can find a variety of items.

Using a Discount Code on Amazon

To use a discount code on your Amazon order, go to your cart in the upper right-hand corner of your screen. While there, you can review your order before clicking the Proceed to Checkout button in the upper right-hand part of that screen. On the checkout screen in section 2, there is a box where you can enter a discount code. Simply enter the code, hit Apply, and your savings will be reflected in your subtotal on the next screen.

Amazon Prime

In addition to being able to access Amazon Primes massive catalog of music, TV, and movies, you can consistently save money on Amazon purchases. All Prime members are eligible for free two-day shipping, but select ZIP codes have the option of same-day delivery and even two-hour delivery at no additional charge. Being a member of Prime also lets you participate in Prime Wardrobe and Prime Pantry, which both have their own list of benefits when shopping for food or clothing.

Does Amazon Offer Free Shipping?

Items that are sold and shipped by Amazon are eligible for free shipping as long as your order includes at least $25 worth of those items. Amazon Prime members get free two-day shipping on Prime orders.

Amazon Return Policy

Items shipped from Amazon can typically be returned within 30 days of purchase, although select departments may have different rules. To return an item, go to the top of the screen to the Returns & Orders tab. There is a step-by-step process that walks you through the return process.

How Do You Contact Amazon Customer Service?

You can call Amazons customer service number at 1 (888) 280-4331. They also offer a live-chat option on their customer service page.

How To Apply an Amazon Promo Code

  1. Go to Amazon.com, and add the items you wish to purchase to your shopping cart by clicking the "Add to Cart" button.
  2. Once you have selected all your products and want to check out, click on the shopping cart logo at the top right corner of the screen. You can also click the "Proceed to Checkout" button once you have selected your last item.
  3. Go to the "Select Your Payment Method" screen, and enter your payment information.
  4. Then, scroll down to find the "Have a gift certificate or promotional code?" box. Type or paste your Amazon.com code in this box and hit "Apply."
  5. You should notice the discount applied to your order total immediately after using a valid promotional code.
